⚠️ Program notes & calf guardrails
Slow circuit order
Squat → rest → Press → rest → Pull → longer rest. Complete ALL rungs of one ladder before starting the next ladder.
Heavy / Medium / Light
These refer to VOLUME (reps/ladders), not weight. Your TRM weight stays fixed for all 8 weeks. Only the rep totals change.
Rest between sets
~1 min after 2-rep sets · ~1 min after 4-rep sets · ~1–2 min after 6-rep sets · 2–3 min between full ladders.
Calf zero-load rule
Goblet squat to a box only. No ankle drive on press. If calf tightens mid-set, stop and rest. Flat feet on swings.
Swing days are easy
Max explosive effort per rep but low total fatigue. 15 sets of 7 OTOM. Add 20–30 min kayak after if feeling good.
Week 7 deload / Week 8 test
Week 7: reduced volume, same weight. Week 8: one pattern tested per session. Log your retest in the TRM tab.
TRM Testing — Find Your Working Weight
For each movement pattern, perform reps at each KB weight with perfect technique. Your 8–10 TRM is the heaviest weight where you hit 8–10 clean reps to technical failure. This weight is fixed for all 8 weeks — the volume changes, not the weight.
⚠️ Test each pattern on a separate day with full rest. Only testing 35 and 50 lb — your working weight will fall in that range. The 20 lb KB is reserved for windmills, get-ups, and warm-up use only.
